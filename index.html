Welcome to the CompSoc Capture The Flag Competition!

On this page we will just go over a few rules, once read you can sign up to the leaderboard system. When you do this please don't use a real passcode. It is only for authentication so no one cheats, but this is just a quick web app so can never be too careful ;)

Rules:

  1. Do not attempt to hack or attack the domain/server that this website is on. This is just a helpful little thing I cooked up to make things easier for you.
  2. You can use any online resource you think could be helpful for this competition as long as other participants would have equal access. (So no paid services, books or otherwise paywell blocked content)
  3. You will have three hours to complete as many tasks as possible
  4. The person or team with the most point at the end of the compeititon wins.
  5. Not all challenges are weighted equally.
